What are carbon offsets and allowances? +

Carbon offsets are tradable certificates and proof that a specific amount of CO2 has been removed from the atmosphere. One ton of carbon offset represents one ton of greenhouses gases removed from the atmosphere.

A carbon allowance is a certification or a permit, which gives its holder the right to emit one metric ton of carbon dioxide or an equivalent greenhouse gas.
